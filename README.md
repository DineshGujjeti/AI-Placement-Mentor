## 📖 Overview

AI Placement Mentor is an intelligent career guidance platform designed to assist students in their placement preparation journey.

Many students struggle with:

* ❌ Lack of structured placement guidance
* ❌ Skill gap identification
* ❌ Interview preparation
* ❌ Aptitude planning
* ❌ Resume optimization

AI Placement Mentor addresses these challenges through personalized recommendations, learning roadmaps, and readiness assessment.

---

## 🚨 Problem Statement

Students often face difficulties during placement preparation due to a lack of mentorship, structured learning plans, and awareness of industry expectations.

This results in:

* Skill gaps
* Poor interview performance
* Reduced placement opportunities
* Lack of confidence

The project aims to bridge this gap using Artificial Intelligence and personalized guidance.

---

## 💡 Solution

AI Placement Mentor acts as a virtual placement coach that:

✅ Analyzes student profiles

✅ Identifies skill gaps

✅ Generates personalized roadmaps

✅ Suggests aptitude preparation plans

✅ Provides interview practice questions

✅ Reviews resumes

✅ Calculates placement readiness score
